No Intro Required

By Samuel Giddins on Friday, June 22nd, 2012

If you’re insane enough to be reading this, then you probably have a sinking suspicion that you don’t need to. And you’re probably right. Except I think you should stay anyway. I might not be a supergenius or a hilarious comedic, but I think I have something to offer. As such, I want you to judge me on what I post here, not who I am. Because that’s germane to the discussion. With that said, here are some of my creds:

  • Rising Senior in High School

  • Trumpeter, Flugelhorn player

  • Bassist, Guitarist, Singer

  • Apple Enthusiast

  • Aspiring Developer

  • Writer

  • Cynic and Pundit

With that out of the way, let’s get started!
